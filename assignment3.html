Delicious Pancakes

These pancakes are light and fluffy, perfect for a Sunday morning breakfast.

Total Time: 30 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter
  • 1 egg

Preparation

  1. Mix all dry ingredients together in a bowl.

    5 minutes

  2. Whisk wet ingredients in a separate bowl.

    3 minutes

  3. Combine wet and dry ingredients, and mix until smooth.

    2 minutes

  4. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat.

    1 minute

  5. Pour batter onto skillet and cook until bubbles form on the surface.

    2 minutes

  6. Flip and cook until golden brown.

    2 minutes
